Overview

We at "DEMO" Hospitals believe that no one deserves to stay disable for long. Our high-end diagnostic facilities which includes superior rehab techniques and Laminar airflow supported OTs for orthopaedic & joint replacement surgeries, enable us to minimize your days of disability.

Our internationally reputed team of advanced trauma care and joint replacement specialists make us one of the most reliable orthopaedic care centres in East India. We believe in providing the most effective diagnosis for all orthopaedic complications from trauma and degeneration to sports injury and other disorders.

The department at the Bhubaneswar unit specializes in the treatment of musculoskeletal system disorders. Our areas of expertise include arthritis, spine surgery, orthopaedic oncology, paediatric orthopaedics, musculoskeletal tumour surgery and reconstruction, rheumatology, hand, ankle, foot among others. We are also specialists when it comes to Paediatric orthopaedic surgeries like congenital deformity.

Our wide array of orthopaedic & joint replacement services includes:

• Trauma Surgery
• Joint and Spine Replacement
• Reconstruction Procedures
• Arthroscopy
• Sports Medicine
• Paediatric surgery
• Deformity correction
