Overview

We take great pride in offering comprehensive care facilities for infants and children. Our multi-disciplinary team of highly experienced paediatricians, neonatologists, surgeons, therapists and nurses across different specialties ensure that children across various age groups receive the best possible care and treatment.

Care is provided in dedicated & fully equipped Neonatal ICU, Paediatric ICU & Paediatric ward. Our 23 bed capacity Paediatric ward is totally separated from adult area as per internationally accepted healthcare guidelines. AMRI Mukundapur also offers highly specialized Emergency Retrieval service for sick neonates & children.The department of Paediatrics & Neonatology at the Mukundapur unit can be associated with the following:

• Fully dedicated & well equipped PICU
• State of the art Blood Gas Machine with Lactate Value
• 24 hrs. Renal Replacement Therapy
• 24 hrs. Ultrasound & Echocardiography
• Blood Pressure Monitoring facility in Ambulance
• Paediatric Bronchoscope Unit
• Children's play areas both in paediatric ward and outpatient areas


Paediatric Uro-nephrology

Our Mukundapur unit is renowned for the highly specialized uro-Nephro care that it offers. Apart from all general Nephro Uro services of this department, we also provide sub specialty clinics Nephrotic Syndrome, Bladder problems etc.The main highlights include:

• International standard Renal Biopsy under deep sedation with Real Time Ultrasound Guidance
• Paediatric Uro Dynamics study for complicated Bladder problems.
• Dedicated unit for Nephrotic Syndrome
• Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitoring in all Paediatric age group.
