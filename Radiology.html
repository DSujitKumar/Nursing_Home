Stroke Treatment

The Mukundapur unit of "DEMO" provides the most advanced, 24x7 facilities for stroke management. It comprises of the best stroke neurologists, neuro-surgeons, and rehab team who ensure that the treatment that patients receive is of the highest order. The digital Cath Lab enables the carrying out of highly advanced treatment procedures like IV thrombolysis and Thrombectomy. Apart from this, a lot of emphasis is also laid on stroke prevention, where high risk groups are advised to act in advance.
