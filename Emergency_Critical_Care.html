Emergency & Critical Care

Vitalizing patient health with prompt care

Overview

We at "DEMO" understand that prompt care and expertise are paramount while dealing with potentially life-threatening situations. Being equipped with one of the best critical care units in East India enable us to provide the most reliable and efficient service on a 24x7 basis.

State-of-the-art technologies & equipment, highly experienced critical care doctors, and super-skilled nurses and technicians have made "DEMO" a ‘go-to’ option for everybody seeking emergency and critical care. We understand that every second is critical for the patient. We have large Intensive Care Units, Intensive Therapy Units and a fleet of critical care ambulances that ensure that not a single moment is lost.

Over the years, we have developed quite an expertise in dealing with emergency, critical care and trauma management. Each year, thousands of patients are admitted to out Intensive Care Units (ICU). "DEMO" is renowned to have the most number of critical care beds in Bhubaneswar. The first and the fifth floor has a total of 131 beds with the average area of the rooms being over 320 square feet. "DEMO" Mukundapur has the largest Neonatal Intensive Care Unit in the private sector in Eastern India. We do everything in our control to ensure that the patients who are admitted into the emergency and critical care unit receive medical and nursing care of the highest order.
