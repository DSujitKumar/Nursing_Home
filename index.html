Who we are...

Welcome to "DEMO" Hospital where the experience of around two decades of providing first - class care is at your disposal.

Conceived and planned as a multispecialty healthcare center, the hospital has been a pioneer in service excellence thereby ensuring patient satisfaction. Located in the heart of the temple city of Bhubaneswar, we aim to provide comprehensive healthcare service under one roof at an affordable price to patient from all socioeconomic strata. Whether you are coming for treatment or bringing new life into the world, we are committed to making your visit both comfortable and expedient.

Inculcation of qualities such as perceptive listening and careful observation among our personnel and a pledge to put the patients’ interests above our own are the hallmarks of our service. This commitment is assimilated into our dynamic workforce and their passion to gain patient’s trust is reflected in the goodwill the hospital has garnered for itself.

Our endeavour is to make modern healthcare facilities within the reach of one and all.
